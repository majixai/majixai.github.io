Shipping Protocols

Select "In-Store Pickup" at checkout to collect your order from our physical location. You will receive an email notification when your order is ready. Please bring a copy of your order confirmation and a valid ID.

Location: 123 Jinx Gen Way, Suite 100, Tech City, 12345

Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

We offer local delivery within a 20-mile radius of our store. Deliveries are made on Tuesdays and Fridays between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM. A delivery fee of $15 will be applied to your order. You will be contacted to arrange a specific delivery window.

We ship to all locations within the United States. Orders are typically processed within 2 business days. Standard shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on the weight and destination of your order. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.
