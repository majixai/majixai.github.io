Sample Daily Schedule Template (6-7 Year Olds)

Date: _______________

Time Slot Activity/Subject Details/Notes
Morning Session (Example: 3 hours)
8:00 - 8:15 AM Arrival & Welcome Circle Songs, prayer, sharing time

10:45 - 11:00 AM Music & Movement Singing hymns/songs, actions, simple instruments
Optional Mid-day Break / Transition (If full day)
11:00 - 12:00 PM Lunch & Quiet Play/Reading e.g., Eat together, then quiet reading or drawing
Afternoon Session (Example: 3 hours for a 6-hour day)
12:00 - 12:45 PM Christian Values/Ethics Story, discussion, role-playing (e.g., kindness, sharing)
12:45 - 1:30 PM Nature/Creation Study OR Project Time Outdoor observation, science experiment, or work on a longer-term project
1:30 - 1:45 PM Short Break / Active Game e.g., Stretching, simple group game
1:45 - 2:30 PM Review & Application / Special Activity Review day's learning, application activity, guest speaker, or themed game
2:30 - 2:45 PM Clean Up & Pack Up Tidy classroom, gather belongings
2:45 - 3:00 PM Closing Circle & Dismissal Prayer, song, reminders

Notes for Flexibility (2-8 hour day):

  • Shorter Days (2-4 hours):
    • Combine or shorten segments.
    • Focus on core subjects: Bible Story, Literacy/Numeracy basics, one hands-on activity (Art/Music).
    • Example 2-hour day: Welcome (10 min), Bible Story (20 min), Literacy/Numeracy (30 min), Craft/Music (30 min), Snack/Break (15 min), Closing (15 min).
  • Longer Days (5-8 hours):
    • Expand time for each subject.
    • Include more project-based learning.
    • Add dedicated time for:
      • Free choice centers
      • Service project planning/execution
      • Deeper exploration of topics
      • More breaks / outdoor play
    • Can include a "quiet hour" for reading or individual work.
  • Remember to adjust based on the children's energy levels and engagement.
  • Integrate themes across subjects where possible.
