Note for Mrs. Scott: Adapt this quiz for the specific age sub-group. For 2-3 year olds, assessment is primarily observational; a formal quiz is not appropriate. For 4-5 year olds, use very simple picture matching, oral questions, or pointing. For 5-6 year olds, the examples below might be suitable with support. For 7-8 year olds, you can increase complexity, add simple written answers, or more questions.
