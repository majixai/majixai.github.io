Bible Theme 4: The Life of Jesus

Overall Summary of the Life of Jesus

Many years after the prophets, God fulfilled His biggest promise! He sent His own Son, Jesus, into the world. Jesus was born in a humble stable in Bethlehem. Angels sang and shepherds came to worship Him. Wise men followed a special star to bring Him gifts. This was Christmas!

When Jesus grew up, He began His ministry, teaching people about God's love and the Kingdom of Heaven. He chose twelve special helpers called disciples to learn from Him and help Him. Jesus taught in amazing ways, often using stories called parables, like the story of the Good Samaritan or the Lost Sheep, to help people understand important truths about God and how to live.

Jesus also performed many incredible miracles that showed He was truly God's Son. He healed people who were sick, helped blind people see, and even calmed a huge storm with just His words! One time, He fed over five thousand people with just five loaves of bread and two small fish. Everywhere Jesus went, He showed compassion, love, and God's power.

He taught people the most important commandments: to love God with all their heart, soul, and mind, and to love their neighbor as themselves. Jesus's whole life was about showing God's love to the world and inviting people to follow Him.
