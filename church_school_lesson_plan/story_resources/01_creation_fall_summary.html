Bible Theme 1: Creation & The Fall

Overall Summary of Creation & The Fall

In the very beginning, there was nothing but God. Then, God decided to create everything! He spoke, and light appeared. He made the sky, the land, and the seas. He filled the world with amazing plants, shining stars, swimming fish, flying birds, and all sorts of wonderful animals. Everything God made was good and beautiful.

Finally, God made the first people, Adam and Eve. He made them special, in His own image, to take care of His beautiful world and to be His friends. God placed them in a perfect garden called Eden. There was only one rule: they were not to eat fruit from one special tree, the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil.

But a sneaky serpent (Satan) tricked Eve into eating the fruit, and she gave some to Adam. When they ate it, they disobeyed God. This was the first sin, and it brought sadness, trouble, and separation from God into the world. Adam and Eve had to leave the perfect garden. But even though they made a mistake, God still loved them and had a plan to one day fix what was broken and bring people back to Him.
