Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be happy with your purchase. You can return products for any reason, whether they are defective or you've simply changed your mind. We currently accept returns from the United States.

Exchanges are also available. Please contact us to arrange an exchange.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must be in new, unused condition.

You have 1 day from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Holiday Return Policy

For orders placed between December 14, 2025, and January 1, 2026, we have an extended return window of 7 days.

How to Make a Return

You can return your items using one of the following methods:

  • In-store
  • By mail
  • At a designated drop-off location

We provide a free, printable return label for your convenience. There is no restocking fee for returned items.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, please allow up to 10 days for us to process your refund to the original form of payment.
