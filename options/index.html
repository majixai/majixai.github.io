1-Leg Strategies

These involve buying or selling a single call or put option contract.

2-Leg Strategies (Spreads)

Combine two different option contracts on the same underlying asset, often to define risk or express a more nuanced market view.

3-Leg Strategies

Involve three option contracts, often used for specific price targets or volatility plays like butterflies.

4-Leg Strategies

Complex strategies using four option contracts, frequently employed for range-bound markets (like Condors, Butterflies) or volatility plays, typically offering defined risk.
