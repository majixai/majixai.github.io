# Dime Defense

## Base Dime

**Play:** Cover 4 (Quarters)

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** Long yardage situations, "Hail Mary"
*   **Field Location:** Own 40 to own endzone
*   **Hash:** Middle of the field
*   **Time Clock:** End of half or end of game

**Play:** Prevent Defense

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 4th and very long
*   **Field Location:** Anywhere on the field
*   **Hash:** Any
*   **Time Clock:** Last minute of the game when protecting a lead
