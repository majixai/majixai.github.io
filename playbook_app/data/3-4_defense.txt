# 3-4 Defense

## Base 3-4

**Play:** Zone Blitz

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 2nd and 7-10, 3rd and 3-6
*   **Field Location:** Anywhere on the field
*   **Hash:** Any
*   **Time Clock:** Any quarter, good for confusing the offense

**Play:** Stunt 1

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 3rd and 10+
*   **Field Location:** Opponent's 40 to opponent's 20
*   **Hash:** Any
*   **Time Clock:** 3rd or 4th quarter, when you need a sack
