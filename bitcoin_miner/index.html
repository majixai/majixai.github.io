Bitcoin Mining Demo EDUCATIONAL

A real SHA-256 proof-of-work simulator running entirely in your browser. No real Bitcoin is mined. No external connections are made.

How Bitcoin mining works: Miners repeatedly hash a block header (data + nonce) with SHA-256 until the result starts with enough leading zeros — the difficulty target. Finding that nonce is pure trial-and-error, illustrating why real-world mining requires specialised hardware. Adjust the difficulty slider to experience this first-hand.
