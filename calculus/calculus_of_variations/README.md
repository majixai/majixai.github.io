# Calculus of Variations

## Scope

Optimization of functionals over spaces of curves/functions, connecting
analysis, mechanics, PDEs, and control.

## Core Topics

- First variation and Euler-Lagrange equations
- Natural boundary/transversality conditions
- Isoperimetric and constrained variational problems
- Second variation, Legendre condition, Jacobi fields
- Noether symmetries and conservation laws
- Direct method in the calculus of variations

## Canonical Results

- Euler-Lagrange theorem
- Noether theorem
- Weierstrass-Erdmann corner conditions
- Existence via coercivity + lower semicontinuity (direct method)

## Typical Applications

- Geodesic and minimal-surface problems
- Variational PDE derivations (weak forms)
- Optimal control links via Pontryagin/Hamiltonian formalisms
