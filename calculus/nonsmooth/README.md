# Nonsmooth Calculus

## Scope

Generalized derivative frameworks for convex and locally Lipschitz functions,
critical in modern optimization and variational analysis.

## Core Topics

- Convex subdifferentials and monotone operators
- Clarke generalized gradient and directional derivatives
- Proximal mappings and Moreau envelopes
- Variational inequalities and complementarity structures
- Epigraphical convergence and stability of minimizers

## Canonical Results

- Fenchel-Moreau duality
- Subgradient calculus rules under regularity assumptions
- Minty-Browder principles for monotone inclusions

## Typical Applications

- Large-scale convex optimization methods
- Composite objective modeling in machine learning
- Contact/friction mechanics and equilibrium problems
