# Stochastic Calculus

## Scope

Differential calculus for stochastic processes, especially semimartingales and
Brownian-driven dynamics.

## Core Topics

- Quadratic variation and semimartingale decomposition
- Itô integral construction and martingale properties
- Itô formula in scalar and vector settings
- Stratonovich integral and geometric interpretation
- Stochastic differential equations and generators
- Fokker-Planck / Kolmogorov equations

## Canonical Results

- Itô isometry
- Existence/uniqueness for Lipschitz SDE coefficients
- Girsanov transform basics

## Typical Applications

- Quantitative finance diffusion models
- Filtering and stochastic control
- Random perturbations of mechanical systems
