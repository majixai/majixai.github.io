# Nickel Defense

## Base Nickel

**Play:** Cover 3

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** Obvious passing situations, 3rd and long
*   **Field Location:** Anywhere on the field
*   **Hash:** Any
*   **Time Clock:** Any quarter, especially when the opponent is trying to come back.

**Play:** Corner Blitz

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 2nd or 3rd and short
*   **Field Location:** Red zone
*   **Hash:** Wide side of the field
*   **Time Clock:** Any quarter, good surprise play
