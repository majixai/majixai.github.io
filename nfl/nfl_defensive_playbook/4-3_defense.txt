# 4-3 Defense

## Base 4-3

**Play:** Cover 2

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 1st and 10, 2nd and 5-10
*   **Field Location:** Own 20 to opponent's 40
*   **Hash:** Middle of the field
*   **Time Clock:** 1st or 2nd quarter

**Play:** Man-to-Man Blitz

*   **Recommended Down and Distance:** 3rd and 5-8
*   **Field Location:** Opponent's 30 to opponent's 10
*   **Hash:** Any
*   **Time Clock:** 4th quarter, game on the line
