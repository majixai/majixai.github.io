Quantum Market: Interactive Guide

Welcome to the Quantum Market Engine

This system is a powerful, automated market analysis tool. It uses a combination of quantitative modeling and generative AI to fetch, analyze, and predict market movements for a user-defined watchlist.

Core Concepts

  • The Heartbeat (minutelyHeartbeat): This is the main engine cycle. Every minute, it fetches live data, runs a Monte Carlo simulation to project future prices, analyzes its own error, and saves the results.
  • The Daily Oracle (runDailyPredictionRoutine): Before the market opens, this process uses AI to analyze the previous day's entire data log and make specific OPEN and CLOSE predictions for the day ahead.
  • Email Data Loop: An advanced feature for extreme data payloads. It can package gigabytes of historical data into a compressed, multi-part email series (called "Data Shards") and then reassemble it, ensuring data integrity with a cryptographic hash. This allows the system to move massive datasets between different environments or for backup.

Understanding the Data

  • Price: The last fetched price from Google Finance.
  • Projected: The price projection for the next ~15 minutes based on the Monte Carlo simulation.
  • Sigma (œÉ): A measure of historical volatility. Higher sigma means the asset is more unpredictable.
  • Error: How wrong the last projection was. The system uses this to learn and become more accurate over time.
